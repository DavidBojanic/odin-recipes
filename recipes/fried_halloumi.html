Fried Halloumi

Description

Slices of halloumi cheese are pan-fried in olive oil and finished with fresh lemon juice and oregano for a tangy, salty, and savory saganaki cheese dish. Serve this appetizer with crusty bread for mopping up all the delicious pan juices!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive olive
  • 1 package halloumi cheese
  • 1 large lemon, juiced
  • 2 pinches dried oregano

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet big enough to fit all halloumi slices.
  2. Once oil is hot but not smoking, add halloumi.
  3. Cook until well browned on the bottom, about 4 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook the other side until browned, about 3 minutes.
  5. Pour lemon juice over browned halloumi and let reduce slightly.
  6. Remove from heat and sprinkle with oregano. Serve hot.
